The Basics of Scrum

Scrum is a simple process that allows teams to build products incrementally in iterations, to keep abreast of the changing market needs and align themselves to the organizations business goals.

It also allows us to focus on delivering the highest business value in the shortest time. This is designed for teams with fewer than 10 dividing works into goals to complete within a time frame called sprints, from 2-4 weeks.

The Scrum team evaluate the development of work every day with stand-up meetings (Daily Scrum) maximum of 15 minutes.
